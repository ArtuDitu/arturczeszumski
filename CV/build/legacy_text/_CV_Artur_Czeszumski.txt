Artur Czeszumski
Profile
Artur Czeszumski has a Ph.D. degree in Cognitive Science and Psychology at
the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam & University of Osnabrück. His research topics
include social, computational, and cognitive neuroscience, cognitive and clinical
psychology, and embodied cognition. He has a strong background in data analysis
(data modeling, data visualization and communication). He is passionate about using
data and technology in football. Currently, he works as a data scientist in football
club Legia Warszawa.
