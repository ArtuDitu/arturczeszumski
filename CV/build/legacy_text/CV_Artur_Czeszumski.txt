Artur Czeszumski has a Ph.D. degree in Cognitive Science and Psychology from
the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam & University of Osnabrück. His research topics
include social, computational, and cognitive neuroscience, cognitive psychology, and
embodied cognition. He has a strong background in data analysis (data modeling, data
visualization, and communication). Currently, he works as a post-doctoral researcher
at the University of Bielefeld. His work is focused on building an open database and
dashboard to perform live analysis on many datasets.
