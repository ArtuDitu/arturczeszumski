Artur Czeszumski has a Ph.D. degree in Cognitive Science (University of
Osnabrück) and Psychology (Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam). His research topics
include social, computational, and cognitive neuroscience, cognitive and clinical
psychology, and embodied cognition. He has a strong background in data analysis
(data modeling, data visualization and communication). He is driven by integrating
computational modeling with rigorous behavioral and neurophysiological experimenation to
better understand human cognition in real-world. Currently, he works as a data
scientist in football club Legia Warszawa and as lecturer at University of Warsaw.
